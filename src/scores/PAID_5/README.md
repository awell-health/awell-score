# Problem Areas In Diabetes - Short Form (PAID-5)

## Introduction

The Problem Areas In Diabetes (PAID-5) is a 5-item short form of the PAID-20 scale. It focuses on understanding how diabetes has impacted the participant's life, including diabetes-associated injuries, lifestyle changes, and medical care.

## Questions and Scoring

Respondents are asked: "Which of the following diabetes issues are currently a problem for you? Please select the option that gives the best answer for you."

| Question | Not a problem | Minor problem | Moderate problem | Somewhat serious problem | Serious problem |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Feeling scared when you think about living with diabetes | 0 | 1 | 2 | 3 | 4 |
| Feeling depressed when you think about living with diabetes | 0 | 1 | 2 | 3 | 4 |
| Worrying about the future and the possibility of serious complications | 0 | 1 | 2 | 3 | 4 |
| Feeling that diabetes is taking up too much of your mental and physical energy every day | 0 | 1 | 2 | 3 | 4 |
| Coping with complications of diabetes | 0 | 1 | 2 | 3 | 4 |

## Calculation

The total score is calculated by summing the scores of all 5 items (range: 0-20).

## Interpretation

A total score of >= 8 indicates possible diabetes-related emotional distress, which warrants further assessment.
